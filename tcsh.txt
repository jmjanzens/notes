### csh foreach loop (like for loop in bash)
foreach i (`seq 1 5`)
echo $i
end

### alias foreach loop
alias f 'foreach i (`seq 1 30`)\                                                                                                                                                                            echo $i\                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        end'

end'
